| Option | Shortcut | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Hide sidebar | Ctrl + K | Hides or shows the sidebar on the left side of the screen. You can also use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + K to toggle this option. |
| Hide information panel | Ctrl + L | Hides or shows the bottom panel of the screen, which displays the details and charts related to the top panel. You can also use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + L to toggle this option. |
