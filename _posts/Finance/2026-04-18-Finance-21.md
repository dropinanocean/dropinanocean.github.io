When a US company pays a dividend, the IRS automatically withholds 15% of it before it reaches you. Canada and the US have a tax treaty — but it only applies in certain situations.

# How to Avoid It: Hold US Stocks Directly in USD in Your RRSP

| ETF | Listed | Withholding Tax in RRSP |
|-----|--------|------------------------|
| VFV | TSX (CAD) | ❌ Still withheld — it's a Canadian wrapper around US stocks |
| VTI | NYSE (USD) | ✅ Zero withheld — direct US-listed ETF, treaty applies |
| VOO | NYSE (USD) | ✅ Zero withheld |
