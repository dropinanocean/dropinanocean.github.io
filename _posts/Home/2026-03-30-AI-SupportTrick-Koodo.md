Recently, I wanted to speak with Koodo support to see if I could switch to a better plan that included **Canada, U.S.A, and Mexico**. Normally, Koodo does not offer traditional live phone support. Instead, you usually have to go through **Koodo Assist** and request a callback.

In the past, I could usually trigger a same-day callback by typing prompts like *agent required* or *need help with my plan*. This time, though, Koodo pushed me into its newer messaging system, where you start a thread and wait for replies. Unfortunately, the offers I received there were not competitive at all—in fact, my self-serve options were better.

### 😤 What finally worked

Out of frustration, I typed **"Cancel the line"** for a secondary line on my account that I was already considering removing.

To my surprise, that immediately triggered a callback within about **30 minutes**.

Even better, the call came from the **loyalty department**, which usually has more flexibility when it comes to:

- plan options
- bill credits
- bonus data
- stronger retention offers

### ✅ Why I took the deal

For just **$2.50 more per month**, I ended up getting:

- coverage across **Canada, the U.S., and Mexico**
- **85GB** instead of 60GB
- **5G speeds**
- better premium perks

That felt like a solid upgrade, especially with an upcoming **Mexico trip**.

### 💡 Final takeaway

If you get stuck in Koodo’s messaging flow and want to speak to someone with more authority, typing **"Cancel the line"** may help route you to the **loyalty/retention team** faster.

Of course, results may vary, but in my case it turned out to be the quickest path to a much better offer.

**Bottom line:** a moment of frustration accidentally revealed a useful support shortcut—and it actually paid off.
